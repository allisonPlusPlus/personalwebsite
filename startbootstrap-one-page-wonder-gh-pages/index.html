The Woman Behind the Code

Hello friends my name is Allison and I’m aspiring web developer from Berkeley, California. I’m a proud member of Women who Code and Lesbians Who Tech. When I’m not coding I love seeing musicals, meeting new people, and watching cat videos on the internet.

What Would You Do If You Weren't Afraid?

While I enjoy my day-job in technology as an Account Executive, I have craved the opportunity to pursue my passion for making beautiful websites and applications. So in April of 2017 I applied and I was accepted to be a Software Engineering Apprentice at Learner's Guild in Oakland, California. My goal is to become a full-time working full-stack developer.
